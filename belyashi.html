Belyashi

Belyashi are fried pies filled with juicy minced beef or lamb, popular across Central Asia and loved for their crisp crust and flavorful meat inside.

prep time

60 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

1-2

Tags : Central Asian cuisine Fried pies Belyashi Street food

instructions

step 1

Prepare yeast dough by mixing warm water (or milk), sugar, yeast, flour, and salt. Knead and let rise until doubled.

step 2

Mix minced meat with chopped onion, salt, and pepper. Divide the dough into small balls, flatten them, and place meat filling in the center.

step 3

Fold edges, leaving a small hole in the middle. Fry in hot oil until golden on both sides and fully cooked inside.
