Pigodi

Pigodi are soft steamed buns filled with a savory cabbage and meat mixture â€” a popular dish of Korean communities in Central Asia. They are fluffy, juicy, and perfect for sharing.

prep time

50 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

minimum 1

Tags : Steamed buns Pigodi Central Asian Street food

instructions

step 1

Prepare the dough by mixing flour, warm water, yeast, sugar, salt, and a little oil. Knead until smooth and let it rise until doubled. Meanwhile, make the filling by mixing ground meat with cabbage, onion, salt, pepper, and oil.

step 2

Divide the dough into small balls, roll each one out, and place a spoonful of filling in the center. Fold and pinch the edges to seal into round buns.

step 3

Place the buns in a steamer, cover, and steam for about 20-25 minutes until fully cooked. Serve warm.
