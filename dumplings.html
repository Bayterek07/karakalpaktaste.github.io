Dumplings

Dumplings is a traditional Central Asian made with thin wheat dough and finely chopped meat filling. These small triangular dumplings are boiled in a savory meat broth and served hot, often seasoned with herbs, black pepper, and sometimes yogurt or sour milk

prep time

45 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

minimum 1

Tags : pelmeni dumplings maindish traditional

instructions

step 1

Prepare the dough by mixing flour, egg, salt, and a little water. Knead until smooth and elastic, then roll into thin squares. Meanwhile, finely chop the meat and onions, mix them with salt and pepper to create the filling.

step 2

Place a small spoonful of filling in the center of each dough square. Fold the square into a triangle, pinch the edges tightly, and join the two bottom corners to form traditional dumpling shapes.Make sure the filling is fully enclosed.

step 3

Bring a pot of water or meat broth to a boil. Gently add the dumplings and cook until they float and are fully cooked (usually 5-8 minutes). Remove carefully, serve hot in the broth, and garnish with herbs or a side of yogurt if desired.
