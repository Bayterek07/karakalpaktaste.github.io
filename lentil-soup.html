Lentil soup

A warm and comforting lentil soup made with red lentils, onions, carrots, and fragrant spices — simple, nutritious, and perfect for everyday meals.

prep time

25 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

minimum 1

Tags : Soup Quick & easy maindish Central Asian cuisine

instructions

step 1

Rinse the lentils well.Heat oil in a pot, add chopped onion and carrot,and sauté until soft and fragrant

step 2

Add tomato paste (if using), stir, then pour in water or broth. Add lentils, salt, spices, and bring to a boil.

step 3

Reduce heat and simmer until the lentils are soft and the soup thickens. Stir occasionally. Serve with fresh herbs.

ingredients

Red lentils

Water or broth

Onion, finely chopped

Carrot, diced

Vegetable oil or butter

Tomato paste (optional)

Salt

Black pepper

Turmeric or cumin (optional)

Fresh herbs for serving (dill, parsley)
