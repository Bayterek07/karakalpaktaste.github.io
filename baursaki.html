Baursaki

Barsaki are small deep-fried dough pieces, crispy outside and soft inside, commonly served with tea or as a side to soups in Karakalpak cuisine.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

12 min.

serving

minimum 1

Tags : baursak Fried dough Karakalpak cuisine Traditional snacks

instructions

step 1

Mix flour, water (or milk), salt, and optionally an egg to form a soft dough. Knead until smooth and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.

step 2

Roll out the dough to medium thickness and cut into small diamond-shaped or square pieces.

step 3

Deep-fry the pieces in hot oil until golden and crispy. Drain and serve warm.

ingredients

Wheat flaour

Sugar (optional, for slightly sweet barsaki)

Vegetable oil (for frying)
