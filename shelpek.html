Shelpek

Shelpek is a thin, round fried flatbread traditionally served with tea or used as a simple everyday bread

prep time

15 min.

cook time

12 min.

serving

minimum 1

Tags : shelpek Fried bread Karakalpak cuisine Traditional snacks

instructions

step 1

Mix flour, water (or milk), salt, and egg if used. Knead into a smooth dough and let rest for 10-15 minutes.

step 2

Divide the dough into portions and roll each into a thin round flatbread.

step 3

Fry the rounds in hot oil until golden on both sides. Serve warm.

ingredients

Wheat flaour

Water or milk

Salt

Vegetable oil (for frying)
