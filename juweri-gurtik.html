Juweri Gurtik

Juweri Gurtik is a traditional Karakalpak dish made from layers of thin dough filled with meat, onions, and spices. It is carefully folded and baked or fried until golden, creating a savory and flavorful treat often served at family gatherings and special occasions.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

minimum 3

Tags : Juweri meat maindish traditional

instructions

step 1

Prepare the dough using sorghum (jugar) flour, salt, and hot water â€” knead until firm and shape into round dumplings or patties.

step 2

In a pot, boil meat with onions, carrots, and potatoes to create a rich broth. Then gently add the shaped dough pieces into the boiling broth.

step 3

Simmer until the dough is cooked through and the flavors of the meat and vegetables meld. Serve the Juweri Gurtik warm, as a hearty stew.

ingredients

2 cups sorghum (jugar) flour or all-purpose flour

1 cup water (hot)

1 tsp salt

300-400 g meat (beef or lamb), finely chopped

1-2 onions, finely chopped

1-2 carrots, chopped

1-2 potatoes, diced (optional)

2-3 tbsp vegetable oil

Water or broth for boiling

Optional: black pepper or other spices to taste
