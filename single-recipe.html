Plov

Karakalpak plov is a filling rice dish made with meat, carrots, and spices. It is often served at parties and family meals, giving warmth,taste, and a feeling of togetherness. Every family has its own recipe, passed down for generations, making plov a special part of Karakalpak hospitality and culture.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

minimum 2

Tags : rice meat maindish traditional

instructions

step 1

Heat the oil and lamb fat in a large pot until hot. Add the onions and fry until golden. Add the beef and cook until lightly browned.

step 2

Add the carrots and cook for a few minutes. Season with salt, cumin, turmeric,and both kinds of raisins. Pour in water to cover the meat and let it simmer to develop flavor.

step 3

Rinse the rice thoroughly and spread it evenly over the top. Do not stir. Add water just enough to cover the rice, then cook on high heat. Reduce to low and steam until the rice is tender and the water has evaporated.

ingredients

500 g carrots

4 onions

100 g lamb tail fat (kurdyuk)

400 ml sunflower oil

350 g beef

500 g rice

1 tsp cumin

1 tbsp yellow raisins

1 tbsp black raisins

1 tsp turmeric

Salt to taste

Water as needed
