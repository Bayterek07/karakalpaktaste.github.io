Funchoza

Funchoza is a light and flavorful noodle dish made with glass noodles, stir-fried vegetables, and seasoned meat. It's fresh, colorful, and popular at family gatherings in Central Asia.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

minimum 1

Tags : Funchoza Salad Central Asian Noodle dish

instructions

step 1

Soak the glass noodles in hot water until soft,then drain and set aside.Prepare the sliced meat and vegetables.

step 2

Heat oil in a pan and stir-fry the meat until browned. Add onion, carrot, bell pepper, garlic, salt, pepper, and soy sauce. Cook until vegetables soften slightly.

step 3

Add the noodles to the pan, mix well, adjust seasoning, and cook for 1-2 minutes. Serve warm or chilled.

ingredients

Glass noodles (mung bean noodles)

300-400 g beef or chicken, thinly sliced

1 large carrot, julienned

1 bell pepper, sliced

1 onion, thinly sliced

2-3 garlic cloves, minced

Soy sauce

Vegetable oil

Salt & black pepper (to taste)

Optional: chili flakes, sesame seeds, fresh herbs (dill or parsley)
